About UFLC

On 1st July, 2018 students from various colleges across Pune came together to form UFLC. It evolved to be a common platform for youth ignited with social responsibility. We adopted a democratic setup for organization so that ever capable and committed person gets to play a vital role through UFLC.

We have conducted more than 25 social events and projects within a span of 15 months either individually or through collaborations. UFLC is open to all kinds of collaborations for social benefit. We have also worked together with various colleges across Pune. Currently we are active in various spheres of work through field work, awareness and campaigns. UFLC is open for new ideas & projects and is determined in driving this movement to a larger scale.

Our Mission

We Aim To

Developing a responsible and vigilant society for countering social issues and strengthening our democracy.

Involving more & more educated and professional youngsters into the social movements.

Playing a role in countering environmental issues by addressing local environmental issues.

Educating people about democratic rights and responsibilities. Providing them a platform to perform the same.

Working for gender and communal equality & unity.

Engaging in skill development and psychological development of students.

To give voice to vulnerable section of society.

To develop a platform where people could contribute to social work by investing minimum time and money.
